About Us

The Center for Strategy and Policy Innovation (CSPI) is a Metropolitan University's academic strategy research and training center dedicated to doing research and providing consulting services in the fields of policy, negotiation, and strategy. The primary objective of the CSPI is to engage in the development, evaluation, analysis, and advancement of strategy-building for action-based policies to enhance the well-being of the state and broader society.
